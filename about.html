ABOUT ME

Lesli Anderson

Lesli Anderson, Graphic Designer, Marketing & Events – Lesli was born in Missoula, Montana, raised on the Flathead Indian Reservation, and graduated from Ronan High School. She continued her education at Montana State University (Graphic Design), Salish Kootenai College (Digital Art & Design, Media Design), and currently at the University of Montana (Media Arts). Lesli has an Associate’s degree in Media Design and Certification in Digital Arts & Design and is pursuing her Bachelor’s Degree in Media Arts. Lesli has worked professionally as a Graphic Designer since 2006 with work for the Valley Journal and then later at D & D Customs SignPro. She has served the Salish Kootenai College campus professionally as Student Service Staff with Center for Prevention and Wellness; Institutional Fundraiser with the SKC Foundation; and, is currently in a leadership role as Department Head and full-time faculty of the Digital Design Technology Department. Lesli has also dedicated many hours to service projects within the larger Flathead Reservation communities. Some of these projects include working with SKC programs on a variety of projects, local health departments and hospitals on health outreach, Mission Valley Lights Under the Big Sky, the Local Chamber of Commerces, numerous local small businesses and non-profit organizations.
